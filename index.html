This application is still being developed and should not be used for route planning at this time.

This Bridge Vertical Clearance Trip Planner is a guide for vertical clearances, but because of physical changes to highways and other possible inconsistencies due to new construction, pavement overlays, lane reconfiguration, etc, it cannot be guaranteed. As stated on all permits, the operator is responsible to clear all obstructions. WAC 468-38-070 states It is the responsibility of the permit applicant to check, or prerun, the proposed route and provide for safe maneuvers around the obstruction or detours as necessary. Note that the lane in which the maximum and minimum clearances occur are not listed. This must be determined by the operator. Also note that the clearances displayed are approximately three inches less than the actual measured clearances.
